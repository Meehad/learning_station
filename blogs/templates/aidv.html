What is AI/ML?

AI (Artificial Intelligence) refers to the development of intelligent machines that can perform tasks that typically require human-level intelligence. ML (Machine Learning) is a subfield of AI that focuses on developing
algorithms and statistical models that enable machines to learn from and make predictions or
decisions based on data. This is done by providing machines with training data and allowing them to
learn from that data without being explicitly programmed. ML is used in a wide range of
applications, including image and speech recognition, natural language processing, recommendation
systems, and predictive analytics.

How to learn AI and ML?

  1. Start with the basics of programming, statistics, and linear algebra.
  2. Learn the fundamentals of AI/ML and the different types of algorithms.
  3. Practice with small projects and gradually increase the complexity.
  4. Join groups or forums to interact with others in the industry, ask questions,
    share projects, get advice from experts, and keep up with the latest news.
