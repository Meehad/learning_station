What is UI/UX?

User Experience (UX) is the overall experience a user has when interacting with a product or service. It
encompasses all aspects of the user's interaction, from usability and accessibility to design and satisfaction.
The goal of UX design is to create intuitive, user-friendly interfaces and workflows that allow users to
accomplish their goals efficiently and effectively. To achieve this, UX design requires a deep understanding
of user behaviour, needs, and preferences, and continuous testing and feedback to ensure the best
possible experience. Ultimately, the purpose of UX design is to create a positive experience for the user,
leading to increased engagement, retention, and loyalty.

How to learn UI/UX?

  1. Read books and articles
  2. Take online courses and tutorials
  3. Practice design exercises
  4. Attend UX design events
  5. Get feedback
  6. Work on real projects
  7. Join communities to connect with peers, ask questions, share projects, learn from others in the field, & stay up-to-date on new developments.
