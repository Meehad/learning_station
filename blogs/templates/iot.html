IoT

-By Alhina

What is IoT?

IoT, or Internet of Things, is a network of physical objects, devices, and machines that are embedded
with sensors, software, and connectivity to collect and exchange data. This data can be used to automate
processes, improve efficiency, and provide insights into how systems are operating, resulting in increased
productivity, cost savings, and improved decision-making.

How to learn IoT?

  1. Start with electronics, networking, and programming fundamentals to understand the underlying technologies powering IoT devices.
  2. Choose an IoT platform or development board like Arduino, Raspberry Pi, or NodeMCU for hands-on experience.
  3. Explore sensors and actuators to collect data and control physical devices. Common sensors include temperature, humidity, and motion sensors.
  4. Develop IoT applications, starting with simple projects like smart home devices, or complex systems such as industrial automation solutions
  5. Join communities to connect with peers, ask questions, share projects, learn from others in the field, & stay up-to-date on new developments.
