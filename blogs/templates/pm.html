What is Product Management?

Product management is the process of identifying, creating, and managing a product from ideation to
launch and beyond. It involves market research, product strategy, roadmap planning, and
collaboration with cross-functional teams to deliver a successful product that meets customer needs.

How to learn Product Management?

  1. Learn the basics through reading books, blogs, and articles
  2. Seek out mentors who can guide and share their expertise
  3. Gain hands-on experience through side projects, internships, or volunteer opportunities.
  4. Attend workshops and conferences to learn about the latest trends and network with other product managers.
  5. Join communities or forums to connect with peers, ask questions, share, learn from others in the field, and stay up-to-date on new developments
