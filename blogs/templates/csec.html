Cybersecurity

-By Ayisha

What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of safeguarding computer systems, networks, and digital information from unauthorized access, theft, damage, or malicious activity. It includes technologies, processes, and policies designed to protect against cyber attacks, data breaches, and other security threats. As organizations rely more on technology and store sensitive data online, cybersecurity is becoming increasingly important.

How to learn Cybersecurity?

  1. Learn computer basics, networking, and common cyber attacks.
  2. Study cybersecurity framework components: prevention, detection, response, recovery.
  3. Grasp security technologies and tools used for computer and network security
  4. Explore popular cybersecurity programming languages like Python and JavaScript.
  5. Gain practical cybersecurity skills through challenges, competitions, and simulations.
  6. Keep current with cybersecurity news and trends by following industry blogs, forums, and conferences.
  7. Join communities to connect with peers, ask questions, share projects, learn from others in the field, & stay up-to-date on new developments.
